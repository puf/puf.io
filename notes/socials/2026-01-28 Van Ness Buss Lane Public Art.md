The Van Ness Bus Rapid Transit (BRT) is a 2-mile, dedicated bus-lane on Van Ness Avenue (US 101) from Mission street to North Point street. Its main feature is the center-running red-colored lane over which busses zoom me to my destination, but when I walk along Van Ness I also appreciate the public art that was part of the project, which opened in 2022.

![lollipop statues](https://i.imgur.com/1TlqW6l.png)
