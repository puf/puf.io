This statue in the [Hearts in San Francisco](/tags/hearts-in-san-francisco) series is called [Fibers of Life][fibers] and was made by San Francisco artist The Apex (real name: Ricardo Richey) in 2024. 

![pic of plaque](https://i.imgur.com/fhzsRUg.png)

You can currently find it in [China Basin Park][chinabasinpark] in Mission Bay, right across from the Giants ballpark.

![pic of heart with ballpark in the background](https://i.imgur.com/4Wzm3bo.png)
